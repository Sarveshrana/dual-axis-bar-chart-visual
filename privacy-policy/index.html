This Privacy Policy describes how the Dual Axis Grouped Bar Chart Power BI custom visual handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

This visual does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data of any kind.

The visual receives data exclusively from the Power BI data model you connect to it. This data is used solely to render the chart on screen and is never transmitted outside of your Power BI session.
